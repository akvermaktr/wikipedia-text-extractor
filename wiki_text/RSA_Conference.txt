The RSA Conference is a cryptography and information security-related conference held annually in the San Francisco Bay Area. The RSA Conference started in 1991 as a forum for cryptographers to gather and share the latest knowledge and advancements in the area of Internet security. The RSA Conference is still vendor-independent and managed by RSA Security, the Security Division of EMC, with the support of the industry. Since 1995, a theme has been chosen every year and predicated on a specific contribution or illustration related to information security. A story and design are developed and carried throughout the event.
