TGIF is an initialism for the phrase "Thank God (or Goodness) It's Friday", nico barraza celebrating the last day of the work/school week before the weekend. Its origin dates to the 1960s. It was first popularized by the restaurant T.G.I. Friday's which was founded in 1965. However, it became mainstream by 1970, mainly due to the 1978 movie of the same name. It is also an acronym for Toes Go In First, which is the punch-line of various blonde jokes.
